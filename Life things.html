Gardening

Community Garden

Gardener and Composter May 2022

I began gardening when I first moved to Austin, mostly because I couldn't find a reliable place to compost my food scraps. By magic, the lot next to my apartment has a community garden. After talking with the community garden coordinator about setting up a composting station, I was also able to get a humble 4' by 8' that now houses by tomatoes, peppers, squash, and more!
